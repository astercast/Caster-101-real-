Our Story

The aWizard Collective

@aWizardxch

The aWizard Collective is a close-knit, community-first project on the Chia blockchain (XCH), built around NFTs, tokens, education, generous rewards, and meaningful collaborations. It focuses on growth magic ‚Äî sprouts, love, wizards ‚Äî incentivized participation, and leaving holders better off through sustained value.

Origins: Ethereum Beginnings

2022

The story starts in 2022 on Ethereum, where core members connected through vibrant NFT communities. They met via mints, discords, giveaways, spaces, traits discussions, and art collaborations ‚Äî building friendships and exploring web3 together. Members brought all sorts of experiences, knowledge, and talents to the table. Constantly connecting on live X spaces and participating in the same ecosystems the Wizards stuck together.

It was in this environment that aWizardDao was born as a small, grassroots DAO. Starting modestly, it provided educational resources, community chats, and project links ‚Äî serving as an early hub for learning and collaboration. This laid the foundation for the collective's emphasis on transparency, onboarding, and shared growth. NFTs were minted here as the very first access tokens to the same Discord we use today. The same Base multi-sig wallet we use now was set up back then with initial High Council members.

Transition to Chia

Embracing Sustainability

Later, the group shifted focus to Chia's greener, proof-of-space-and-time model ‚Äî drawn to its regulatory alignment, security, enterprise-readiness, and real-world utility potential. This move allowed the DAO and community to evolve on a more sustainable chain, migrating ideas, assets, and energy to XCH while retaining Ethereum roots.

On Chia, aWizard remained intentionally small-scale at first, prioritizing quality connections and real contributions over mass adoption.
