🧙‍♂️ Our Story

The aWizard Collective is a close-knit, community-first project on the Chia blockchain (XCH), built around NFTs, tokens, education, generous rewards, and meaningful collaborations. It focuses on growth magic — sprouts, love, wizards — incentivized participation, and leaving holders better off through sustained value.

🌐 Origins: Ethereum (2022)

The story starts in 2022 on Ethereum, where core members connected through vibrant NFT communities — mints, discords, giveaways, spaces, and art collaborations. Building real friendships and exploring web3 together laid the foundation for everything that followed.

aWizardDao was born as a small, grassroots DAO providing educational resources, community chats, and project links. The same Base multi-sig wallet and Discord we use today were set up during this era.

🌱 Transition to Chia

The group shifted to Chia's greener proof-of-space-and-time model — drawn to its regulatory alignment, security model, enterprise-readiness, and real-world utility. Ideas, assets, and energy migrated to XCH while retaining our Ethereum roots.

On Chia, aWizard stayed intentionally small-scale at first — prioritizing quality connections and real contributions over chasing mass adoption. That patience became our identity.

✨ Key Milestones
❤️‍🔥
$LOVE Token & Love Bear NFTs

An emoji token and companion Love Bear NFT collection launched as core community utility — powering rewards programs, liquidity pool incentives, and ongoing community engagement. $LOVE became the heartbeat of the on-chain aWizard experience.

🖼️
aWizard NFT Collection

The flagship NFT collection delivering real perks — private Discord access, priority drops, and ongoing holder incentives. Designed to reward loyalty and deepen community ties rather than just speculate on floor prices.

🌱
$SPROUT — "Seeding Potential Randomness Over Usage Time"

Random surprise airdrops to NFT holders, rewarding patience and long-term holding over trading. The unpredictability became part of the fun — you never knew when your wallet would sprout. It grew into one of the most beloved CAT tokens in the Chia ecosystem.

🤖
Custom Discord Tooling

Built a suite of community-facing tools: real-time on-chain portfolio snapshots, bulk airdrop mechanisms, automated soft-staking drip systems, and a Spell Power Leaderboard that gamified participation and rewarded the most active holders.

📚
Education & Ecosystem Onboarding

Hosted regular X Spaces, wrote educational threads, and ran collaborative initiatives to help newcomers navigate Chia and web3 broadly. Growing the ecosystem one dedicated member at a time was always the mission — not quick flips.

🔐
Sage Wallet — Beta & Custom Theme

Among the earliest beta testers of Sage Wallet, providing detailed feedback to help shape the product. We also designed, built, and minted a fully custom aWizard theme for the wallet — one of the first community-created skins for Chia's leading wallet.

🔮
$CASTER — Fall 2025

Launched as the "dimensional shift-onchain-magic asset" of the Caster universe — a token designed to tie the whole wizard ecosystem together on Base. Went live just before #Castober, paired with aggressive Merkl liquidity campaigns. The first #Castober saw 57 $CASTER burned and 1/1 hand-drawn NFTs given away to participants. Combines the Chia heritage with Base-native DeFi.

📜
First Memecoin Option Contract on Chia

aWizard launched what is believed to be the first memecoin option contract on the Chia blockchain — not a traditional financial instrument, but an on-chain primitive specifically built for the memecoin community. It allowed $CASTER holders to lock in future purchase rights at a set price, bringing structured financial tooling to Chia's memecoin space in a community-first way.

🎁
Airdrops & Ongoing Rewards

Thousands of dollars in emoji tokens and $XCH distributed through generous direct airdrops, Merkl liquidity rewards, and weekly NFT prize draws. The community has always been the priority — rewarding presence and loyalty over passive holding.

🤝
Cross-Project Collaborations

Built real relationships across the Chia ecosystem — Tang Gang, VorobaXCH, MonkeyZoo, PizzaOnChia and others. These weren't just promotions; they were mutual support arrangements that brought new members to aWizard and donations to the treasury.

🌉
Bridging $BYC to Base Before Circuit DAO

aWizard bridged Bytecash ($BYC) from Chia to Base — launching it as Wizard Bucks — before Circuit DAO launched their official bridge. We did this using our own bridge fork, making us the first to bring $BYC cross-chain. It was a pioneering move for community-driven cross-chain asset migration in the Chia memecoin space.

🎵
Music & Culture

Albums and music releases during bear periods kept morale high and the wizard lore alive. Wizard Wednesdays on X became a recurring ritual — a touchpoint that kept the narrative continuous even when markets were quiet. Culture over charts.

💎
New Soft Staking Token — 2026

A brand-new token launching in 2026 aimed at rewarding long-term liquidity providers and powering the next generation of Merkl campaigns. Designed to deepen DeFi participation while maintaining the community-first ethos that defines aWizard.

These elements reflect aWizard's commitment to value-driven growth over hype — burns tighten supply, yield rewards attract and retain liquidity, and the community's culture keeps the magic alive.
